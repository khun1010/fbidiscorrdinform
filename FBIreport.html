How to Report Illegal Activity

Librarian
  • 2 months ago
  • Updated
Not yet followed by anyone

If you encounter illegal content or behavior on Discord, such as threats, harassment, or other criminal activity, you can report it to Discord’s Trust & Safety team and, if necessary, to the FBI. This guide explains how to take action.

What is Illegal Activity?

Illegal activity on Discord includes any behavior or content that violates local, state, or federal laws. This can range from harassment to serious crimes like threats of violence or distribution of illegal materials.

Examples of Reportable Content

Here are some examples of content you should report:

  • Threats of violence or harm
  • Harassment or bullying
  • Child exploitation or abuse material
  • Promotion of terrorism or extremist activities
  • Illegal drug trafficking or other criminal enterprises

Pro Tip: Always take screenshots of illegal content before reporting, as it may be removed by the user or Discord after your report.

How to Report to Discord

To report illegal activity, start by notifying Discord’s Trust & Safety team. They can remove content, suspend users, and escalate issues to law enforcement if needed.

Gathering Evidence

Before reporting, collect evidence to support your claim:

  • Take screenshots of messages, usernames, or server details.
  • Note timestamps and server invite links.
  • Do not delete messages or leave the server, as this could remove evidence.

Submitting a Report

Follow these steps to report to Discord:

  1. Right-click the user or message and select “Report.”
  2. Alternatively, go to Discord’s Trust & Safety form.
  3. Select the appropriate category (e.g., “Illegal Content”).
  4. Provide details and attach screenshots.
  5. Submit the report and keep the confirmation email.

Reporting to the FBI

For serious crimes, such as threats of violence or child exploitation, report directly to the FBI:

  • Online: File a complaint at www.ic3.gov. Include all evidence and details from Discord.
  • In Person: Contact your local FBI field office. Find locations at www.fbi.gov.
  • Emergency: Call 911 or local law enforcement for immediate threats, then inform the FBI.

Note: Discord cooperates with law enforcement for serious issues, but you must report to them first to ensure proper handling.

Next Steps

After reporting, take these steps to stay safe:

  • Block the user to prevent further contact (Blocking Guide).
  • Adjust your privacy settings to limit who can message you.
  • Monitor your account for suspicious activity.
